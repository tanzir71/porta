Honest security

Public by design.
Private when you add a password.

Porta links are hard to guess, but anyone with the URL can reach the share while it is on. Use password protection for sensitive folders, disable uploads unless needed, and stop the share when you are done.

Good to know

Before you open the door.

Does Porta upload my files somewhere?

No Porta storage service exists. While a share is live, files travel from your computer through your selected tunnel provider to people using the link. Stop the share and that tunnel stops serving your folder.

What tunnel providers can I use?

Cloudflare Quick is built in and needs no account. You can also configure a managed Cloudflare tunnel, ngrok, or a compatible custom CLI. Tokens stay in Keychain or Credential Manager, and custom arguments run without a shell.

Why does my computer show a security warning?

The Windows setup is not Authenticode signed, and the macOS app is ad-hoc signed rather than notarized. Follow the platform instructions above only for an installer downloaded from this official repository.

Does closing the window stop my links?

No. Porta keeps running quietly in the system tray. Turn individual shares off there, or choose Quit Porta to stop the app and all active tunnels.

Can I share a local development server?

Yes. Add a port share—for example localhost:3000—and Porta tunnels it directly. Features and production suitability depend on the selected provider; Cloudflare Quick remains intended for testing, not production hosting.
